IT 11 Unit Summary

IT 11 talks about the past, the present, and the future of ICT. It consisted of 7 units, here are the summary for each unit.

Introduction to ICT

This unit tackled an overview on ICT. Also it explored the history of Technology from big computers like UNIVAC to smaller ones like smartphones.

Computers in our Daily Life

This unit discussed the different types of computers and their uses in our daily lives, from personal computers to embedded systems. It also where students learned that 1+1=10. Furthermore, flowcharts and algorithms were introduced here.

The internet and World Wide Web

This unit discussed the history and evolution of the internet and the World Wide Web. It covered topics such as web browsers, search engines, and online communication tools. It also gave tips and tricks for safe and effective internet use.

Information, Control, Ethics

This unit focused on the ethical use of information and communication technology. Topics included data privacy, digital citizenship, and the societal impact of technology. It also introduced different laws and regulations related to ICT in the Philippines.

Culture and Society

This unit explored the impact of technology on culture and society. It started with definitions of literacy then progressed to different types of literacies in ICT such as digital literacy, media literacy, and information literacy. It also discussed the role of technology in shaping cultural norms and values.

Design Principles

This unit covered the fundamental principles of design in technology, including usability, accessibility, and user experience. It emphasized the importance of creating intuitive and inclusive digital products. it also included branding which tackled logo design, color theory, and typography.

Trends, Issues, and Challenges

This unit examined current trends, issues, and challenges in the field of ICT. Topics included emerging technologies, cybersecurity threats, and the digital divide. This was supposed to be a separate unit but was merged with Unit 6 due to time constraints and how similar the topics were.
